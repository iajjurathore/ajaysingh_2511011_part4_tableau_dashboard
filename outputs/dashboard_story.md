### Task 9: Write Dashboard Story


## 1. Executive Summary

Our retail business generated total revenue of **₹217,017,652** across **4,200 orders** over the past two years, with a net profit of **₹33,306,313** representing a **15.35% profit margin**. The business is fundamentally healthy, driven by strong Technology category performance and a growing Home Office customer segment. However, three critical areas require immediate leadership attention — excessive discounting eroding margins, high furniture return rates, and Standard Class shipping delays affecting customer experience.

---

## 2. What is Performing Well

**Technology Category is the Growth Engine:**
Technology products — Copiers, Accessories, Phones, and Machines — collectively generate ₹27.9M in profit, accounting for nearly 84% of total business profit. Copiers alone contribute ₹7.3M, making them the single most profitable sub-category.

**South Region Leads All Markets:**
The South region consistently outperforms other regions with ₹64.7M in sales and ₹9.99M in profit. States like Telangana and Kerala are driving strong demand across all customer segments.

**Home Office Segment Shows Strong Revenue:**
Home Office customers generate the highest revenue at ₹74.5M with a healthy profit of ₹11.6M. This segment's growth aligns with the post-pandemic remote work trend and presents a significant opportunity.

**Same Day and First Class Shipping Deliver Excellence:**
Same Day delivery averages just 0.40 days and First Class averages 1.77 days, with minimal delayed orders — demonstrating operational excellence in premium shipping tiers.

---

## 3. What is Underperforming

**Office Supplies Profitability is Critically Low:**
Despite contributing to order volume, Office Supplies generates only ₹1.7M in total profit. Sub-categories like Paper (₹318K), Binders (₹325K), and Labels (₹368K) are high-volume but low-margin products dragging overall profitability.

**East and West Regions Lag Behind:**
East (₹48.9M) and West (₹48.9M) regions significantly underperform compared to South (₹64.7M). These regions have untapped potential that is not being converted into revenue.

**Standard Class Shipping Performance is Poor:**
Standard Class handles 2,435 orders (58% of total) but averages 4.71 delivery days with the highest proportion of delayed deliveries. This directly impacts customer satisfaction and repeat purchase rates.

---

## 4. What Risks are Visible

**Discount-Driven Profit Erosion:**
Orders with discounts above 20-30% frequently result in negative profit. The scatter plot clearly shows that heavy discounting destroys value — some orders at 30%+ discount generate losses of up to ₹20,000 per order.

**High Furniture Return Rate:**
Furniture category has a 7.67% return rate — more than double the Technology return rate (3.03%). In East region, Home Office Furniture returns reach a critical 12.63%, indicating a systemic quality or expectation mismatch.

**Revenue Seasonality Risk:**
Sales show significant month-to-month volatility, dropping as low as ₹6M in some months while peaking above ₹10M in others. This unpredictability makes cash flow management and inventory planning difficult.

---

## 5. What Opportunities are Visible

**Technology Expansion Opportunity:**
Given Technology's superior profit margins, expanding the product range and increasing marketing investment in Copiers and Accessories could significantly boost overall profitability.

**Home Office Segment Growth:**
The Home Office segment's strong performance indicates growing demand from remote workers. Targeted product bundles — combining Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies — could increase Average Order Value beyond the current ₹51,522.

**South Region Replication Strategy:**
The South region's success model can be studied and replicated in East and West regions. Identifying top-performing cities in South and mirroring those strategies elsewhere could unlock ₹10-15M in additional revenue.

**Premium Shipping Upsell:**
Only 630 orders (15%) use First Class shipping. Offering incentivized upgrades to First Class for high-value orders could improve customer satisfaction and reduce return rates linked to delivery damage.

---

## 6. Recommended Business Actions

1. **Cap discounts at 20%** across all categories and implement a discount approval workflow for orders above 15%
2. **Increase Technology inventory** — especially Copiers and Accessories — to meet growing demand
3. **Launch a Furniture quality review program** to identify root causes of high return rates, particularly in East region
4. **Negotiate better SLAs with Standard Class logistics partners** or incentivize customers to upgrade to faster shipping
5. **Invest in East and West region marketing** by replicating South region's successful sales strategies
6. **Introduce seasonal sales campaigns** during low-performing months to stabilize monthly revenue
7. **Create Home Office product bundles** to increase Average Order Value and customer retention

---

## 7. Limitations of the Dashboard

- **Geographic mapping issue:** Regional performance map shows approximate state-level data. City-level granularity would provide more actionable insights for territory planning.
- **No customer lifetime value data:** The dashboard tracks transactions but cannot identify repeat customers or calculate customer lifetime value.
- **No competitor benchmarking:** All metrics are internal — there is no external benchmark to evaluate whether 15.35% profit margin is industry-competitive.
- **Campaign channel ROI missing:** While campaign channels (Organic, Social, Paid, Referral, Email) are tracked, there is no revenue attribution analysis to measure marketing ROI.
- **No real-time data:** Dashboard reflects historical data up to September 2025 and does not update in real time.

---

## 8. Suggested Next Analysis

1. **Customer Lifetime Value Analysis** — Identify high-value repeat customers and build retention strategies
2. **Campaign Channel ROI Analysis** — Determine which marketing channels (Organic, Paid, Social) generate the highest return
3. **Furniture Return Root Cause Analysis** — Deep dive into East region Home Office returns to identify product, delivery, or expectation issues
4. **Price Elasticity Study** — Analyze how different discount levels affect order volume vs profit to find the optimal discount threshold
5. **City-Level Performance Analysis** — Identify top 10 and bottom 10 cities by profit margin to guide expansion and contraction decisions
6. **Seasonal Demand Forecasting** — Build a predictive model to anticipate high and low demand months for better inventory management
